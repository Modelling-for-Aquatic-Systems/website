The MAST research group offers Master thesis opportunities at the intersection of marine science, mathematical modeling, and computational methods. Topics span machine learning and AI for ocean emulation, data assimilation and inverse problems, ecosystem modeling, climate change impacts, and ocean deoxygenation. Students work with state-of-the-art numerical models, real oceanographic data, and high-performance computing, often within European research initiatives and international collaborations.
