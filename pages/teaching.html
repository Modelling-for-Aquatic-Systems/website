I teach several courses at the Master level at the University of Liège, covering mathematical modeling, marine ecosystems, biogeochemistry, and data analysis. My approach emphasizes the integration of mathematical theory with practical applications in marine sciences, encouraging students to develop both analytical and computational skills. Through hands-on projects and numerical simulations, students learn to apply advanced modeling techniques to real-world oceanographic problems, from ecosystem dynamics to climate change impacts on marine environments.
